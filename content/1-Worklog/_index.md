**On this page**, you will introduce your worklog by summarizing the overall process of completing the program. Include how many weeks you spent, what activities you carried out each week, and what you learned or achieved throughout the period.

A typical worklog is usually organized by weekly sections and can be structured as follows:

**Week 1:** [Introduction and overview of the topic or assignment](1.1-week1/)

**Week 2:** [Main tasks and initial progress](1.2-week2/)

**Week 3:** [Further implementation and learning activities](1.3-week3/)

**Week 4:** [Continuing tasks and problem-solving](1.4-week4/)

**Week 5:** [Advanced work and technical development](1.5-week5/)

**Week 6:** [Testing, troubleshooting, or refinement](1.6-week6/)

**Week 7:** [Further development and improvements](1.7-week7/)

**Week 8:** [Additional tasks or project expansion](1.8-week8/)

**Week 9:** [Deepening understanding and practical work](1.9-week9/)

**Week 10:** [Progress review and major milestones](1.10-week10/)

**Week 11:** [Final adjustments and completion of tasks](1.11-week11/)

**Week 12:** [Summary, reflection, and final outcomes](1.12-week12/)
